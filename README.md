## 🧠 About Me

I am a **senior leader in Information and Communication Technology (ICT)** with over **22 years of experience** driving digital transformation, research infrastructure, and capacity building across Africa. Currently serving as **Coordinator of MoRENet (Mozambique Research and Education Network)**, I lead the strategic governance of the national network serving over 100 academic and research institutions.

My career has been dedicated to advancing **digital inclusion**, **scientific collaboration**, and **technological sovereignty** in Africa. I have:
- Founded and led **MoRENet Academy**, training **5,500+ professionals** in cybersecurity, networking, and data science
- Established **MoRENet CSIRT**, the first academic Computer Security Incident Response Team in Mozambique
- Led the deployment of **High-Performance Computing (HPC)** infrastructure in partnership with CHPC (South Africa)
- Represented Mozambique in global forums: **UbuntuNet Alliance and GÉANT**
- Built strategic partnerships with **RNP (Brazil), FCCN (Portugal), ISOC, NSRC (USA), Huawei Mozambique**

I am now advancing my expertise in **Data Science and Artificial Intelligence** through formal training with **IBM** and **WorldQuant University**, combining my deep infrastructure knowledge with analytical and predictive capabilities.

Open to **senior roles in Europe** — in **ICT leadership, data science, HPC, cybersecurity, or research infrastructure** — remote or relocation.
